    HOW I BEGAN AND WHERE I AM HEADED

    My journey began while working crazy hours in Advisory in San Francisco, CA. I discovered an insatiable passion for health and wellness. I aspire to empower others as they move their body and own their potential in a way that is fun. I am certified RYT 200 Hour, Yoga Sculpt, Lagree, and Pilates Instructor.

    Grounded

    I pole-vaulted for my college's Track and Field team. High-impact sports added a lot of physical stress on my body and mind. Ran the 2015 Marine Corp Marathon in 3:48.

    Grateful

    I've learned that the mind-body-connection runs deeper and the power of breath is healing.

    At Peace

    Since 2016, I have taught over 300+ classes (Yoga, Pilates, and Lagree). My mission is to make mindful movements accessible to everyone.
